What Marketing at PLC Technik Looks Like

Industrial automation is a technical field — our marketing team bridges that gap, turning complex engineering capabilities into clear, compelling communication for plant managers, procurement teams, and project decision-makers.

Content Creation

Write website copy, case studies, project summaries, and social media posts that explain what we do in plain language.

Digital Marketing

Manage the company website, LinkedIn presence, and digital campaigns targeting plant engineers and procurement contacts.

Proposal & Presentation Support

Help the engineering and sales teams create polished technical proposals, slide decks, and client-facing materials.

Lead Generation Support

Research potential clients, compile prospect lists, and support the business development team with outreach materials.

Brand Development

Maintain and develop PLC Technik's visual identity across all materials — from project photos to digital assets and printed collateral.

Performance Tracking

Monitor website traffic, social engagement, and campaign results to guide future content and channel investments.

What We Look For

Skills & Background

  • • BS Marketing, Communications, or related degree
  • • Strong writing and editing skills in English and Filipino
  • • Experience with social media management (LinkedIn, Facebook)
  • • Basic graphic design skills (Canva, Adobe tools)
  • • Familiarity with B2B or technical product marketing is a plus

Professional Qualities

  • • Curious about technology and engineering topics
  • • Able to simplify complex technical concepts into clear messaging
  • • Self-directed with strong project ownership
  • • Comfortable working with engineers and technical staff
  • • Eye for detail in visual design and written content
