Greenfield Automation — Mining

Limestone Mine
Sizing Plant Automation

PLC Systems Technik Inc. successfully engineered and implemented a complete automation and SCADA system for a limestone mine sizing plant — delivering a modern control architecture designed to support reliable and efficient mineral processing operations from the ground up.

The project was executed as a full greenfield automation deployment, covering every layer of the control system: PLC engineering, industrial networking infrastructure, instrumentation integration, and supervisory control for the plant's crushing and material sizing operations. Mining environments present unique automation challenges due to dust, vibration, large mechanical equipment, and geographically distributed process equipment. The system was therefore engineered with robust multi-protocol communication, high hardware reliability, and centralized operational visibility as core design requirements.

Scope of Work
Greenfield PLC Engineering SCADA Development Industrial Networking Instrumentation Integration Multi-Protocol Field Integration Mine Site Expansion Fiber Optic Network Alarm Management Historical Trending Commissioning & Handover
Project Phases
Phase 1 — Sizing Plant Automation
January – October 2018

The control system for the sizing plant was built around the Siemens ET200SP distributed PLC platform, selected for its compact design, modular architecture, and powerful processing capabilities suited to industrial process automation in demanding environments.

Supervisory monitoring and plant operation were implemented using Siemens WinCC Professional SCADA, managing approximately 4,649 Power tags across all major plant systems. The SCADA environment provides operators with real-time plant visualization, alarm management, historical trending, and operational diagnostics — enabling efficient monitoring and control of production activities from a centralized interface.

Equipment & Systems Covered
Limestone crushers and feeders
Conveyor systems and transfer points
Screening and sizing equipment
Material flow monitoring systems
Process interlocks and alarm systems
Motor and drive control monitoring
Phase 2 — Mine Site Expansion
Two Years After Phase 1 Commissioning

Following the successful completion of Phase 1, PLC Systems Technik Inc. was engaged again to extend the automation architecture across the broader mine site — covering the extraction and material handling areas that feed into the sizing plant. The mine site automation system scope is currently to be determined as engineering and design activities are ongoing.

A dedicated Siemens WinCC Professional SCADA system was developed for mine site operations and engineered to operate as an integrated extension of the original sizing plant system. Both SCADA environments communicate through a PROFINET-based industrial Ethernet network over fiber optic infrastructure, ensuring reliable high-speed communication between the plant processing systems and mining operations.

Integrated Architecture Enables
Centralized monitoring of plant and mining operations
Coordinated control across extraction and processing
Improved visibility across the full production chain
Enhanced diagnostics and maintenance planning
Scalable infrastructure for future system expansion
Reliable multi-vendor field device integration
