What Engineering at PLC Technik Looks Like

Our engineers are the core of every project — from scope review through FAT, SAT, and handover. You'll own work, not just support it.

PLC Programming

Write and test ladder logic, function blocks, and structured text using Siemens TIA Portal for real industrial processes.

HMI / SCADA Development

Build and configure operator interface screens, alarms, trends, and historian logs using WinCC and panel HMIs.

System Commissioning

Lead loop checks, FAT/SAT execution, instrument calibration, and final handover documentation on-site.

Control Panel Design

Design and assemble industrial control panels — I/O wiring, power distribution, protection devices, and cabinet layout.

Engineering Documentation

Produce I/O lists, cause & effect matrices, P&IDs review, as-built drawings, and commissioning packages.

Troubleshooting & Support

Diagnose and resolve PLC faults, communication failures, and instrumentation issues in operating plant environments.

What We Look For

Technical Skills

  • • Siemens TIA Portal (S7-1200/1500) experience preferred
  • • Understanding of ladder logic and function block diagrams
  • • Knowledge of industrial communication protocols (PROFINET, Modbus)
  • • Familiarity with instrumentation and loop checking
  • • Ability to read electrical and P&ID drawings

Professional Qualities

  • • Licensed Electrical / Electronics Engineer (preferred)
  • • Willing to work on-site in various locations
  • • Strong troubleshooting mindset
  • • Able to work independently and under pressure
  • • Good written and verbal communication
