Greenfield Process Control Engineering for the Mining Industry

Limestone Mine Sizing Plant Automation

Client Industry: Mining & Aggregates | Project Duration: January – October 2018 | System Integrator: PLC Systems Technik Inc. | Completed
Project Overview

PLC Systems Technik Inc. successfully engineered and implemented a complete automation and SCADA system for a limestone mine sizing plant, delivering a modern control architecture designed to support reliable and efficient mineral processing operations.

The project involved the design and deployment of the entire automation system from the ground up, covering PLC engineering, industrial networking, instrumentation integration, and supervisory control for the plant's crushing and material sizing operations.

Mining environments present unique automation challenges due to dust, vibration, large mechanical equipment, and geographically distributed process equipment. The automation system was therefore engineered to provide robust communication, high reliability, and centralized operational visibility.

Automation Platform Architecture

The control system for the sizing plant was built around the Siemens ET200SP distributed PLC platform, selected for its compact design, modular architecture, and powerful processing capabilities suitable for industrial process automation.

The system integrates field devices across multiple industrial communication protocols, including:

01
PROFINET
High-speed industrial Ethernet communication
02
PROFIBUS
Fieldbus communication for legacy and distributed devices
03
AS-i
Integration of distributed sensors and actuators
04
RS485 Serial
Communication with specialized field equipment and instrumentation

This multi-protocol architecture enabled seamless integration of equipment from different vendors across the plant.

SCADA System & Process Monitoring

Supervisory monitoring and plant operation were implemented using Siemens WinCC Professional SCADA, providing operators with a centralized interface to monitor and control plant operations.

The sizing plant SCADA system manages approximately 4,649 Power tags, covering key mining process equipment such as:

Limestone crushers and feeders
Conveyor systems and transfer points
Screening and sizing equipment
Material flow monitoring systems
Process interlocks and alarm systems
Motor and drive control monitoring

The SCADA environment provides operators with real-time plant visualization, alarm management, historical trending, and operational diagnostics, enabling efficient monitoring of production activities.

Expansion: Integrated Mine Site Automation System

Two years after the successful commissioning of the sizing plant automation system, PLC Systems Technik was again engaged to expand the automation architecture to include the Mine Site Automation System, further extending digital control and monitoring across the mining operation.

The mine site automation system includes 247 I/O signals, covering field instrumentation, equipment monitoring, and process control across the extraction and material handling areas of the site.

A new SCADA system was developed using Siemens WinCC Professional, providing supervisory monitoring and control of the mine site operations.

Integrated Plant & Mine Automation Architecture

The mine site system was engineered to operate as an integrated extension of the original sizing plant automation system.

Both SCADA environments communicate through a PROFINET-based industrial Ethernet network over fiber optic infrastructure, ensuring reliable high-speed communication between the plant processing systems and the mining operations.

This integrated architecture enables:

Centralized monitoring of plant and mining operations
Coordinated control between extraction and processing systems
Improved operational visibility across the entire production chain
Enhanced diagnostics and maintenance planning
