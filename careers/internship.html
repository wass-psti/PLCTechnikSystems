What You'll Do

OJT trainees at PLC Technik are treated as junior engineers, not assistants. You'll be assigned to active project teams and expected to contribute.

PLC Panel Familiarization

Observe and assist in wiring, I/O tagging, and cabinet assembly for Siemens S7 systems in our workshop.

TIA Portal Basics

Learn Siemens TIA Portal under engineer supervision — ladder logic, function blocks, and basic HMI screen creation.

Site Commissioning Support

Join engineers on site during commissioning phases — loop checking, instrument calibration, and punch-list documentation.

Technical Documentation

Help prepare I/O lists, as-built drawings, and commissioning reports using real project templates.

SCADA / HMI Exposure

Observe and assist with WinCC SCADA screen configuration, alarm setup, and trending tools.

Team Collaboration

Attend project briefings, ask questions, and work alongside full-time engineers in a real professional environment.
