What Finance at PLC Technik Looks Like

We're a lean, project-driven company. Finance staff work closely with our engineering and management teams — you'll see exactly where the money goes and why it matters.

Project Billing & Invoicing

Prepare and monitor billing milestones tied to project progress, and ensure timely invoicing to clients.

Accounts Payable & Receivable

Manage vendor payments for materials and subcontractors, and track collections from project clients.

Cost Monitoring

Track actual vs. budgeted project costs, flag variances, and support the operations team with cost control data.

Financial Reporting

Prepare monthly financial summaries, cash flow statements, and management reports for leadership review.

Tax & Compliance

Support BIR compliance, VAT filing, payroll processing, and government remittances in coordination with the accounting team.

Cross-Team Coordination

Work directly with project engineers and management to align financial milestones with project delivery schedules.

What We Look For

Skills & Background

  • • BS Accountancy or BS Management Accounting graduate
  • • Familiarity with QuickBooks, Xero, or similar accounting software
  • • Experience with BIR compliance and Philippine tax regulations
  • • Strong Excel / spreadsheet skills
  • • Project-based billing experience is a plus

Professional Qualities

  • • High attention to detail and accuracy
  • • Comfortable working in a small, fast-moving team
  • • Able to manage multiple project accounts simultaneously
  • • Proactive in flagging financial risks or discrepancies
  • • CPA license is an advantage but not required
