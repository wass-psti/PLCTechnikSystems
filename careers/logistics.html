What Logistics at PLC Technik Looks Like

Industrial automation projects depend on precise timing — a missing cable, a delayed instrument, or a misdirected shipment can stall an entire commissioning schedule. Our logistics team makes sure that never happens.

Materials Procurement & Sourcing

Coordinate with engineering teams to source PLCs, cables, instruments, and hardware from local and overseas suppliers at the best price and lead time.

Supplier Coordination

Liaise with vendors and distributors to track orders, resolve delays, and confirm delivery schedules.

Inventory & Warehouse Management

Maintain accurate inventory of project materials, tools, and spare parts in the workshop — ensuring equipment is accounted for and ready for deployment.

Site Delivery & Equipment Mobilization

Coordinate the transport and delivery of control panels, instruments, and tools to project sites — ensuring everything arrives safely and on schedule.

Import & Customs Documentation

Handle import documentation for overseas equipment — coordinating with freight forwarders and customs brokers to clear shipments efficiently.

Logistics Reporting & Cost Tracking

Maintain delivery records, purchase orders, and shipping costs per project — supporting the finance team with accurate materials expenditure data.

What We Look For

Skills & Background

  • • BS Industrial Engineering, Supply Chain, or related degree
  • • Experience in procurement, warehousing, or logistics coordination
  • • Familiarity with import/export and customs procedures in the Philippines
  • • Strong Excel / spreadsheet skills for inventory and cost tracking
  • • Experience with technical or industrial goods is a strong advantage

Professional Qualities

  • • Highly organized with strong attention to detail
  • • Able to juggle multiple projects and deadlines simultaneously
  • • Proactive in flagging supply risks or delivery delays early
  • • Good negotiation skills with vendors and freight partners
  • • Comfortable coordinating across engineering and finance teams
