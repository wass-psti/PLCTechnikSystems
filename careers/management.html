What Management at PLC Technik Looks Like

Our management team keeps projects on track, clients satisfied, and the business growing. You'll work directly with engineers, clients, and company leadership on decisions that matter.

Project Management

Oversee project schedules, resource allocation, deliverable milestones, and client communications from kick-off to handover.

Client Relationship Management

Serve as the primary contact for key clients — managing expectations, resolving issues, and identifying follow-on opportunities.

Business Development

Identify new project opportunities, prepare technical and commercial proposals, and develop relationships with potential clients and partners.

Operations Oversight

Monitor company-wide project performance, resource utilization, and team productivity to ensure on-time, on-budget delivery.

Team Leadership

Coach and develop engineers and support staff, conduct performance reviews, and help build a high-performing team culture.

Strategic Planning

Work with company leadership on annual plans, market positioning, and identifying new service capabilities to invest in.

What We Look For

Background & Experience

  • • 5+ years of experience in engineering, project management, or technical sales
  • • Background in industrial automation, electrical, or process engineering preferred
  • • Experience managing multi-discipline project teams
  • • Track record of client-facing roles and proposal writing
  • • PMP certification is an advantage

Leadership Qualities

  • • Strong communicator with both technical and non-technical audiences
  • • Decisive under pressure with strong problem-solving instincts
  • • Commercially aware — understands project margins and cost drivers
  • • Able to build trust with clients and lead teams without micromanaging
  • • Willing to travel to project sites as needed
